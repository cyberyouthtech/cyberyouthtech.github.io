Cyber Youth Tech (CyTech) has one mission:
raise awareness about cybersecurity.

Cyber Youth Tech (CyTech) is an international, youth-led nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering the next generation of Science Technology Engineering, and Math (STEM) and Cyber leaders. We have established chapters across the United States and the United Kingdom that work together to create a global community dedicated to helping people protect themselves in the cyber realm.

There is a problem in STEM

Despite being an innovate and impacting field, representation in the STEM field is more than lacking. Because of the lack of diversity in this field, it loses out on a broad participation of people that the field would not only benefit from but needs to maintain its legitimacy. To address the gap of an increasing number of underserved communities in the STEM field, CyTech recognizes that not only must work to increase the participation of students in the STEM field, but it must work to specifically help communities that are most in need well before the start of college as these moments are the most crucial.
