Cyber Youth Tech (CyTech) has one clear mission:
raise awareness about cybersecurity

How you can join us?

Please contact one of our leaders listed below or reference our contact page for more information. We are constantly looking for more opportunties to work in local communities and speaking about our mission.

Why do we want to do this?

CyTech was made because we saw the need to educate a world on the importance of cybersecurity and how to protect ourselves as we enter a more technological dependent age.

How are we making a change?

CyTech has taliored made curriculum to introduce people of all ages about Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math (STEM), specificlly in the realm of cybersecurity. While we want to educate people on how to safely trasverse this digital world, we also want to bring more people into the STEM field that otherwise would have never been given the opportunity and help them to succeed by starting programs in areas that are lacking in STEM exploration.
