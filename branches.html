Fullerton, California - USA 
Branch Lead: Kanin Liang

Working in their community whether that be Boys and Girls Club or the YMCA, the Fullerton Branch has done everything from creating activities for their students to teaching coding and cybersecurity classes.

Huntsville, Alabama - USA
Branch Lead: Isabelle Brown

Living in the Rocket City, these Alabamians have been blasting off with their outreach. Visting more than ten schools and going to several technical conferences in the community, our Huntsville chapter has been sharing CyTech's love for education. 

Los Angeles, California - USA 
Branch Lead:  Konan Yutaka

One of our recently founded branches, our Los Angeles Branch is just getting their foot off the ground, getting ready to spread STEM and Cyber education to all people in their community. 

Kent, New Church - UK
Branch Lead: Amberley Odysseas

Our first UK Branch, the Kent Branch works in their school to push for cybersecurity education, having presentations every meeting as well as CyTech's curriculum approved activities.

Parker, Colorado - USA 
Branch Lead: Beth Chwialkowski

The Branch Chapter is actively working for STEM and cybersecurity education in their school that has no pathway for participation in the field. Being our first chapter to do in-school presentations, the Parker Chapter is pushing the boundaries of CyTech education. 

Salisbury, Wiltshire - UK
Branch Lead: Jessica Richards 

With their chapter leader being one of the United Kingdom's highest scoring students in Cyber Discovery, the Salisbury Branch is pushing for cybersecurity education with everything from forensics to cryptography. 

Volga, South Dakota - USA
Branch Lead: Austen King

With a main focus on cybersecurity, the Volga Branch is in its first year, building the foundations for its program as it gets ready to use the CyTech curriculum in their community. 

Yorba Linda, California - USA 
Branch Lead:  Currently Electing 

Our Yorba Linda Branch was the first ever group to give a presentation for CyTech, and they haven't stopped since. From workshops to seminars, Yorba Linda keeps on going.
