Cyber Youth Tech

Cyber Youth Tech, also known as CyTech, is an international, youth-led nonprofit that seeks to nurture the next STEM and Cyber Leaders by bringing computer science and cybersecurity community, education, opportunity, and outreach to all students. Starting with one group of teens desiring to make a change, this organization has expanded to reach over 5,000 people in several chapters across the United States and the United Kingdom. Amongst our expansive network of chapters, CyTech has focused on making an impact which have spawned the creation of our curriculum and STEM activities, our connection of resources for students to cultivate their passions, empowerment and spotlight of our students, and more through or pillars of community, education, opportunity, and outreach.

Wanting to bring STEM and Cyber education to all, CyTech has mainly focused on working with underserved people in their community. We want to bring the opportunities that we have had and spread it to all people. Recognizing the importance of a community to motivate students through a support system, education that may is accessible and understandable to all regardless of background of and walks of life, and opportunities that equip students to use their knowledge and make change to the world.

Mission: Cyber Youth Tech (CyTech) aims to bring youth around the world together to impact their communities, education, opportunity, and more by bringing access to technology to all people regardless of their walk of life.

Our Story

CyTech began in the United States when our founding members realized their potential to make a change. Originally created with the purpose to change the perception around cybersecurity, our organizaion has completely expanded upon its initial mission. Wanting to use their given opportunities and share that with the rest of the world, CyTech brought not only cybersecurity education but Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math (STEM) education to the workshops and events that we promoted and worked on. Bringing attention to not only cybersecurity but also STEM, CyTech has become an interntaional organization that is contiously expanding everyday.

With every line of code or another student taught, CyTech had created a curriculum and program that is bringing about the end of the divide in education and resources. Today, we work across the world to host our interactive presentation and activities in all parts of our community. In our time as an organization, we realized that we wanted bring computing education to all, inspiring future leaders as well as unlocking people to their fullest potential.
