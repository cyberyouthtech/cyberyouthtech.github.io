About Us

Cyber Youth Tech (CyTech) is an international, youth-led nonprofit organization

Our Mission

Cyber Youth Tech (CyTech) as one clear mission: raise awareness about cybersecurity.

The world is becoming susceptible to viruses, malware, hackers, and many more due to our new digital era. In order to prevent defenseless citizens from being vulnerable against malicious attacks in this age, CyTech wants to better prepare these cyber citizens' defenses from all attempts at cyber crime. Currently, CyTech is visting different schools, libraries, and other organizations all over the United States and the United Kingdom.

CyTech is currently developing a taliored made curriculum to introduce people of all ages to Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math (STEM), specifically in the realm of cybersecurity. While we want to educate people on how to safely trasverse this digital world, we also want to bring more people into the STEM field that otherwise would have never been give the opportunity and help them to succeed by starting programs in areas that are lacking in STEM exploration.

Our Beginning



During the summer of 2018, our founders were chosen by the US Embassy in London to represent their respective countries in the Future Cybersecurity Leaders Exchange to share their passion and knowledge about cybersecurity. Through this trip, our founders realized that compared to many others they had resources which allowed them to have been able to pursue their passions and interests in the tech field that many their age and younger did not have. In fact because of this issue, many their age may not understand the what cybersecurity is or even how to protect themselves when using using the internet. Knowing the importance cybersecurity as the modern world is developing new and innovative technologies, our founders decided to start a program that would allow them to educate others that otherwise would not understand the importance of cybersecurity and how to protect themselves from our digital world.
