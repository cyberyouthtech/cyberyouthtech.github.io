Cyber Youth Tech

Cyber Youth Tech, otherwise known as CyTech, is an international, youth-led nonprofit that seeks to nurture the next STEM and Cyber Leaders by bringing computer science and cybersecurity community, education, opportunity, and outreach to all students. Starting with one group of teens desiring to make a change, this organization has expanded to reach over 5,000 people in several chapters across the United States and the United Kingdom. Amongst our expansive network of chapters, CyTech has focused on making an impact which have spawned the creation of our curriculum and STEM activities, our connection of resources for students to cultivate their passions, empowerment and spotlight of our students, and more through or pillars of community, education, opportunity, and outreach.

Wanting to bring STEM and Cyber education to all, CyTech has mainly focused on working with underserved people. Recognizing the importance of a community to motivate students through a support system, education that may is accessible and understandable to all regardless of background of and walks of life, and opportunities that equip students to use their knowledge and make change to the world.

Cyber Youth Tech is an international, youth-led nonprofit organization dedicated

to empowering and cultivating the next generation of STEM and Cyber Leaders

through community, education, opportunity, and outreach.
